chester bennington-the musician

Chester Charles Bennington was an American singer, songwriter, musician, and actor. He served as lead singer for the bands Linkin Park, Dead by Sunrise, Grey Daze, and Stone Temple Pilots. He was widely regarded as one of the top rock vocalists of the 2000s. Bennington first gained prominence as a vocalist following the release of Linkin Park's debut album, Hybrid Theory, in 2000, which became a commercial success. The album was certified Diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America in 2005, making it the best-selling debut album of the decade, as well as one of the few albums ever to hit that many sales. Linkin Park's following studio albums, from Meteora to One More Light, continued the band's success. Linkin Park has sold over 100 million records worldwide.
On July 20, 2017, Bennington was found dead at his home in Palos Verdes Estates, California. His death was ruled as suicide by hanging.

A brief timeline(1976-2017)

  • 1993 : Chester Bennington first began his music carrer with a band called "Sean Dowdell and His Friends?". Bennington and Dowdell moved on and started a new band the same year, called Grey Daze, releasing 3 albums during their run.
  • 1998 : Chester leaves Grey Daze and struggles to find a new band
  • 1999 : Bennington is recruited by the band Xero (has its name changed to Linkin Park later in the year). The band lands a record deal with Warner Bros. Records.
  • 2000 : Linkin Park releases their first album Hybrid Theory. It was a massive success selling 4.8 million copies in its first year alone as well as catapulting the band and its members into mainstream success
  • 2003 : The bands 2nd album Meteora is released and to no ones surprise was a huge success. Began touring with other big named bands such as Hoobastank, P.O.D, Mudvaye and even Metallica.
  • 2010-2012 : Linkin Park releases two more albums in a short span of time, A Thousand Suns and Living Things The band further experiments with a more modern, electronic sound with less hard rock chords
  • 2013 : Veteran band Stone Temple Pilots parted ways with their lead vocalist and recruited Bennington to fill in. Chester said that it has been a lifelong dream to perform with STP. They release a 5 track EP titled High Rise. Chester was in both bands simutaneously and released the 6th album The Hunting Party the following year.
  • 2017 : The band released their seventh album One More Light. On July 20, 2017 Chester Bennington was found dead in his residence by a housekeeper. His death was ruled a suicide.
